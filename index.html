Biography

I am a PhD student working on gravitational-wave cosmology, working with Dr. Rachel Gray and Dr. Chris Messenger. My research focusses in particular are using the population of gravitational wave sources as an independent probe of the Hubble constant, and the optimisation of cosmological inference pipelines.

As part of my research, I am one of the lead developers of gwcosmo, a cosmological pipeline which relies on Bayesian inference.

I also am a member of both the LIGO - Virgo - KAGRA Collaboration (LVK) and the LISA consortium.
