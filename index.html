I am a Scottish PhD student living in Glasgow. My research focuses on using gravitational waves to learn about cosmology and the large-scale structure of the universe. I am particularly interested in Bayesian inference and the development of new models for compact binary coalescence populations.

Outside of physics, I enjoy hiking, fencing, and swimming.
